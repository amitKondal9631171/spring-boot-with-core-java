Architect Interview Questions

- Responsibilities
    Creating a clean architecture based on sound principles.
    Architecture covering all Non Functional Requirements.
    Having good review processes in place for Architecture, Design and Code.
    Ensuring teams are as productive as they can be.
    Right tools.
    Ensuring teams are following the best engineering practices.
    Ensuring clear communication about architecture with business and technical teams.

How do you ensure that the Code Quality is maintained?
    1) Analysis tool like Sonar is the best bet today.
    2) Have good peer review practices in place.
    3) Unit Testing, If a programmer can write a simple and understandable unit test,
        he can definitely write good code.

How do you ensure the team is following sound engineering practices ?
    How often is code committed?
    How often is code released?
    How often is code deployed?
    Are they immediately fixing the issues?
